This is Goneby

With Goneby, you can create rooms and let others know where you are, without actually telling them where you are! Goneby asks you for a short description of your current location, which can then be shared securely with members of your room.

Secure and Private

Security and privacy has always been one of the biggest goals here at Goneby. We use end-to-end encryption for important data, like your login information. No one at Goneby can ever observe your location!

Free and Unlimited

Goneby allows you to create an unlimited amount of rooms, your location can also be different for every room. The feature allows you to create different rooms for various contexts, like family, school or even on trips!

Easy to use

Creating and joining rooms is very easy. In fact, you don't even need complicated links or social logins. Just copy a rooms invitation code to your phones clipboard and share it with family, classmates or colleagues. By default, only the creator of a room has administrative privileges but this can easily be changed by clicking on a member and edit various properties.
